# How to Delete a Product

## Overview
Remove a product from your store permanently. This action cannot be undone.

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Open Admin Dashboard
1. Open browser → `https://your-site.com/admin`
2. Enter admin password → Click **Login**

### Step 2: Go to Products Tab
1. Click the **Products** tab
2. Locate the product you want to remove

### Step 3: Click Delete Button
1. In the product row, look at the **Actions** column
2. Click the **trash icon** (🗑️) — it's the red-highlighted icon next to the pencil icon
3. A browser confirmation popup will appear: **"Delete this product?"**

### Step 4: Confirm Deletion
1. Click **OK** to confirm
   - Or click **Cancel** to abort
2. If confirmed:
   - Success toast "Deleted" appears
   - Product disappears from the table
   - Product is immediately removed from the customer-facing store

### Step 5: Verify Removal
1. Go to store: `https://your-site.com/shop`
2. Search for the deleted product
3. It should no longer appear

---

## Important Warnings

- **Cannot be undone**: Once deleted, the product and all its data (including reviews) are permanently removed
- **Active orders**: If customers have this product in their cart, it will show as unavailable
- **Existing orders**: Past orders that include this product will still show in order history

---

## Alternative: Instead of Deleting

If you want to temporarily hide a product instead of deleting:
1. Edit the product
2. Set **Stock** to `0`
3. The product will show as "Out of Stock" but won't be deleted

---

## Time Required
- ~10 seconds
