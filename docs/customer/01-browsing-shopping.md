# How to Browse & Shop Products (Customer Guide)

## Overview
A complete guide for customers to browse the Snap Aligner store, search for products, use filters, and view product details.

---

## Opening the Store

### Step 1: Visit the Website
1. Open your browser
2. Go to: `https://your-site.com`
3. The homepage loads with:
   - Hero banner ("Engineered for perfect fit")
   - Trust badges (Free Shipping, Warranty, Fast Delivery)
   - Shop by Category section
   - Featured Products grid

---

## Browsing Methods

### Method 1: Browse by Category
1. On the homepage, scroll to **Shop by Category**
2. Click any category:
   - **Tempered Glass** — Screen protectors
   - **Cases** — Phone cases
   - **Holders** — Car mounts, desk stands
   - **Cables & Chargers** — USB cables, chargers, wireless pads
3. The Shop page opens filtered to that category

### Method 2: View All Products
1. Click the **hamburger menu** (☰) in the top-left corner
2. Click **All Products** in the sidebar menu
3. OR click **Shop Now** button on the homepage
4. All products are displayed in a 3-column grid

### Method 3: Click Featured Products
1. On the homepage, scroll to **Featured Products**
2. Click any product card to view its details

---

## Using the Search

### Step 1: Open Search
1. Click the **search icon** (🔍) in the top navigation bar
2. A search input field appears below the navbar

### Step 2: Type Your Query
1. Type what you're looking for:
   - Product name: `"matte case"`
   - Category: `"charger"`
   - Feature: `"wireless"`
2. Click **Search** or press Enter

### Step 3: View Results
1. The Shop page loads with matching products
2. Shows "Results for [your query]" as the heading
3. Shows number of matching products

---

## Using Filters on Shop Page

### Category Filter (Pills)
1. At the top of the shop page, you'll see category pills:
   - **All** | **Tempered Glass** | **Cases** | **Holders** | **Cables & Chargers**
2. Click any pill to filter by that category
3. Active pill is highlighted in blue
4. Click **All** to remove the filter

### Sort Options
1. Click the **Sort** dropdown (top-right)
2. Choose from:
   - **Default** — Newest first
   - **Price: Low to High** — Cheapest first
   - **Price: High to Low** — Most expensive first
   - **Top Rated** — Highest rated first

### Clear Filters
1. Click the **Clear** button (appears when filters are active)
2. All filters and search are reset

---

## Understanding Product Cards

Each product card shows:
- **Product Image** — Main product photo
- **Trust Badges**: 
  - 🔵 "INT'L BRAND" — International quality
  - 🛡️ "PRECISION FIT" — Exact fit guarantee
- **Category** — Small text above the name
- **Product Name** — Bold product title
- **Price** — Current selling price (₹)
- **Compare At** — ~~Strikethrough~~ original price
- **Discount** — Green "XX% OFF" badge
- **Rating** — Star rating with review count
- **Stock Badge**:
  - 🟡 "LOW STOCK" — Only 5-20 left
  - 🔴 "ALMOST GONE" — Less than 5 left
- **Quick Add** button — Add to cart without opening product page

---

## Viewing Product Details

### Step 1: Click a Product
1. Click anywhere on the product card (image or name)
2. The product detail page opens

### Step 2: What You'll See
- **Large product image**
- **Trust badges** (International Brand, Precision Fit)
- **Category** and **Product Name**
- **Star rating** with review count
- **Price** with Compare At strikethrough and discount percentage
- **Description** — Full product details
- **Quantity selector** — Plus/minus buttons
- **Add to Cart** button (blue)
- **Wishlist** button (heart icon)
- **Shipping info** — Free shipping on ₹500+
- **Warranty info** — 1-year warranty
- **Customer Reviews** section

### Step 3: Navigate Back
1. Click **← Back** at the top to return to the shop
2. Or use browser's back button

---

## Using the Sidebar Menu

### Opening the Menu
1. Click the **hamburger icon** (☰) in the top-left of the navbar
2. Sidebar slides in from the left

### Menu Contents
- **Home** — Back to homepage
- **All Products** — Full product catalog
- **Tempered Glass** — Category filter
- **Cases** — Category filter
- **Holders** — Category filter
- **Cables & Chargers** — Category filter
- **Track Order** — Enter order ID to track

### If Logged In (additional options)
- Profile photo and name
- **My Orders** — View order history
- **Admin Panel** (if admin user)
- **Sign Out** button

### Closing the Menu
- Click the **X** button in the sidebar
- Or click the dark overlay area outside the sidebar

## Time Required
- Browse homepage: ~30 seconds
- Search for product: ~10 seconds
- View product details: ~15 seconds
