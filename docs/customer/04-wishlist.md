# How to Use Wishlist

## Overview
Save products you like to your wishlist for later purchase. Requires signing in.

---

## Adding to Wishlist

### Method 1: From Product Card (Shop Page)
1. Browse products on the Shop page
2. Hover over any product card
3. A **heart icon** (♡) appears in the top-right corner
4. Click the heart icon
5. Toast notification: "Added to wishlist"

### Method 2: From Product Detail Page
1. Open any product
2. Click the **heart button** (♡) next to the "Add to Cart" button
3. Toast notification: "Added to wishlist"

### Not Logged In?
- Clicking the heart icon will redirect you to Google login
- After signing in, go back and add again

---

## Viewing Your Wishlist

### Step 1: Open Wishlist
Two ways:

**Way 1: Navbar**
1. Click the **heart icon** (♡) in the top navbar
2. Wishlist page opens

**Way 2: Sidebar Menu**
1. Click hamburger menu (☰)
2. Under your profile, click **Wishlist** (if logged in)

### Step 2: View Products
- All wishlisted products shown in a 3-column grid
- Same product cards as the shop (with price, badges, Quick Add)
- Click any product to view details

---

## Removing from Wishlist

### Method 1: From Product Card
1. On the wishlist page or shop page
2. Hover over the wishlisted product
3. Click the **filled heart icon** (♥) — it will be red
4. Toast notification: "Removed from wishlist"
5. Product disappears from wishlist page

### Method 2: From Product Detail Page
1. Open the wishlisted product
2. Click the **heart button** again
3. Toast notification: "Removed from wishlist"

---

## Wishlist Is Empty?
- Message: "Wishlist is empty"
- Click **Explore** button to go to the shop

---

## Tips
- Wishlist items don't expire
- Use wishlist to compare products before buying
- Quick Add from wishlist page to move items to cart

---

## Time Required
- Add to wishlist: ~3 seconds
- View wishlist: ~2 seconds
- Remove from wishlist: ~3 seconds
