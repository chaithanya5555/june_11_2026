# How to Write & Read Product Reviews

## Overview
Share your experience by writing reviews on products you've purchased. Help other customers make informed decisions.

---

## Writing a Review

### Prerequisites
- You must be **signed in** with Google

### Step 1: Open Product Page
1. Go to any product: `https://your-site.com/product/prod_xxx`
2. Or click a product from the shop/homepage

### Step 2: Scroll to Reviews Section
1. Scroll down below the product details
2. You'll see the **Reviews** section
3. If logged in, a "Write a Review" form appears at the top

### Step 3: Set Your Rating
1. Click on the **stars** (1-5):
   - 1 star = Poor
   - 2 stars = Below Average
   - 3 stars = Average
   - 4 stars = Good
   - 5 stars = Excellent
2. Default is 5 stars
3. Click any star to change the rating

### Step 4: Write Your Comment
1. Click the text box: "Share your experience..."
2. Type your review
3. Tips for good reviews:
   - Mention what you liked/disliked
   - How long you've been using it
   - Whether it fits your phone model
   - Quality of materials

### Step 5: Submit
1. Click the blue **Submit** button
2. Toast notification: "Review added!"
3. Your review appears at the top of the reviews list
4. Product's average rating updates automatically

---

## Reading Reviews

### On Product Page
1. Open any product
2. Scroll to the **Reviews** section
3. Each review shows:
   - **Reviewer's photo** (from Google profile) or initials
   - **Reviewer's name**
   - **Star rating** (1-5 stars)
   - **Comment** text

### Review Count
- Shown in the product header: "★ 4.5 (23 reviews)"
- Also shown on product cards in the shop grid

---

## Tips
- Reviews are public — all visitors can see them
- You can write multiple reviews on the same product
- Reviews cannot be edited or deleted by the customer (admin can manage via database)
- Honest reviews help build trust and improve the store

---

## Not Seeing the Review Form?
- Make sure you're signed in (click Sign In in the navbar)
- The review form only appears for logged-in users

---

## Time Required
- Write a review: ~1 minute
- Read reviews: ~30 seconds
